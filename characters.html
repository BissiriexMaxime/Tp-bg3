Lae'zel has as a warrior class of the Githyanki race. She is independent, curious, and devoted to her queen, she does not particularly like to bond with others

ShadowHeart is a half-elf who belongs to the class of Clerics, she is narrow-minded, prefers pragmatic solutions to problems and does not want to get involved in the problems of others

Gale is a sorcerer-class human. He is an intelligent and thoughtful sorcerer, kind-hearted and peaceful. He has a erudite and slightly pompous style of speech. He speaks openly about himself when asked, describing himself as a «sorcerer of a certain reputation» who loves good wine and occasionally writes poetry.

Astarion is part of the high elven race and as a class, Rogue. He has the appearance of a charming and confident nobleman, endowed with a biting spirit. He likes to make sarcastic remarks and joke at the expense of others, while being smug and haughty.

Wyll is part of the human race and for class, Warlock. Heroic and benevolent, but in conflict and still fighting his pact mistress, Wyll devoted most of his life to fighting on behalf of the people of the Sword Coast. He takes his duty and title seriously and brags about his skills and his past by describing him as «the Frontier Blade».

Karlach is part of the race of tieflings and to for class, Barbarians. She is an extremely passionate person who strives to live her life to the fullest. She tends to talk enthusiastically about all the simple pleasures of life, whether it’s food, drinks, friends or intimacy. Her attitude is generally cheerful and very direct, not being afraid to loudly proclaim her love for being alive and how much she cares about her loved ones. His positive character and contagious energy seem to allow him to easily make friends.

Halsin is part of the Wood Elf race and he has as a class, Druid. Despite his tenacity in battle, Halsin is a gentle and honest elf who cherishes the sanctity and beauty of nature. Though a powerful healer of high standing, Halsin is humble and patient, but willing to act forcefully if the need arises. He considers fleshly pleasures as a natural vocation and monogamy as something that suits others better than him; One of Halsin’s fundamental convictions is that desire must flourish wherever it finds an anchor.

Minthara is part of the drow race and as a class, paladin. Being a drow and follower of the Absolute , Minthara has a very hostile and cruel attitude towards other beings, considering practically everyone as inferior. She has a particular disdain for those who do not share her beliefs and, as a noble drow, she holds the other drow characters in high esteem.

Jaheira is half-elf who has for class, Druid. She’s a stubborn, opinionated woman. Charismatic and insightful, she has a sharp tongue and her mind can be demeaning to those for whom it is intended. She tends to make jokes about serious situations and will approve the player to do so as well. Although she is a veteran of many wars, she retains a healthy sense of humor and does not care to be the target of a good joke.

Minsc is part of the human race and to for class, Ranger. He is a skilled warrior but not particularly intelligent. He thinks that his friend Boo is actually a miniature giant space hamster and that he is the only one who can talk to him. He sees the world in the absolute: if evil exists, it must be defeated.
